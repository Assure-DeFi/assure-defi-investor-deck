01 — The Problem

Crypto BD is broken.
And it's costing the industry billions.

Business development in crypto is structurally broken — whether you're reaching crypto-native projects and tokens, or traditional companies looking to partner in the space. The market is fragmented, pseudonymous, and changing daily. Traditional B2B sales tools were never designed for this world. And the result is a massive, industry-wide BD problem that nobody has solved.

?

No directory exists — for projects or companies

300,000+ crypto projects, tokens, and web3-adjacent companies across 200+ chains and platforms. No LinkedIn. No Salesforce. No comprehensive database. Finding the right prospect — whether it's a new DeFi protocol, a traditional firm entering crypto, or a strategic partner — means manually crawling Twitter, Discord, Telegram, on-chain data, CoinGecko, CMC, and dozens of niche platforms every single day.

✕

You can't find the right person to contact

Even when you find a promising project or company, who do you contact? The main Twitter account? The anonymous founder? The CMO who joined last month? Without team discovery — knowing who holds what role and how to reach them specifically — outreach goes to the wrong person or nowhere at all. Spray-and-pray to a project's main account is not BD. It's noise.

⚠

Generic AI outreach has destroyed cold DMs

"I noticed your innovative project..." is the new "Dear Sir/Madam." Recipients have developed AI-slop radar. Spintax bots flood every inbox. The bar for genuine, research-backed outreach has never been higher — and most tools actively make it worse by sending the same generic message to 10,000 accounts simultaneously.

$

Human BD teams are expensive and structurally inefficient

A 5-person BD team costs $500K+/year. They manually research 10–20 prospects per day. 80% of their time is spent on research, not selling. Most prospects are wrong-fit. The ROI is structurally broken — not because the team is bad, but because the right infrastructure simply doesn't exist to support them.

"We spent 5 years living this problem. We've talked to 1,500+ crypto projects. We tried every tool, every CRM, every VA service. Nothing worked — because the tools weren't built for crypto."

— Jeff Leggett (El Crypto Chapo), Founder & CEO, Assure DeFi

A message crafted from real data, real context, and real relevance — sent to the right person at the right company at the right time.
